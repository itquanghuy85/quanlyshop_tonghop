## Description

### VI

Quan Ly Shop la ung dung quan ly cua hang sua chua va mua ban dien thoai theo huong offline-first, dong bo du lieu theo thoi gian thuc voi Firebase.

Ung dung ho tro tao va theo doi don sua, ban hang, quan ly ton kho, quet ma vach va IMEI, cham cong nhan vien, tinh luong, quan ly khach hang va nha cung cap, in hoa don qua may in Bluetooth, va nhan thong bao day cho cac su kien quan trong. Du lieu duoc tach theo tung shop de giup chu shop va quan ly theo doi hoat dong an toan va ro rang.

Phu hop cho cua hang sua chua dien thoai, cua hang linh kien, cua hang phu kien va cac mo hinh ban le can xu ly don hang, kiem soat ton kho va van hanh nhan su tren cung mot he thong.

### EN

Quan Ly Shop is an offline-first management app for phone repair and retail stores, with real-time Firebase synchronization.

The app supports repair order tracking, sales, inventory management, barcode and IMEI scanning, staff attendance, salary calculation, customer and supplier management, Bluetooth receipt printing, and push notifications for important events. Data is isolated per shop to help owners and managers operate securely and efficiently.

It is suitable for phone repair stores, accessories shops, spare-parts businesses, and other retail workflows that need order handling, stock control, and staff operations in one system.

## App Review Notes

### VI

Ung dung yeu cau dang nhap de su dung. Vui long cung cap tai khoan reviewer co quyen owner hoac manager.

Mot so tinh nang can cap quyen:
- Camera: chup anh may va quet ma vach/IMEI
- Photo Library: chon va luu anh chung tu
- Bluetooth: ket noi may in nhiet
- Location When In Use: cham cong va xac dinh dia diem lam viec
- Notifications: nhan thong bao don hang, chuyen dong nhan su va su kien quan trong

Neu reviewer khong co thiet bi may in Bluetooth, van co the kiem tra cac tinh nang con lai ma khong anh huong den luong chinh cua ung dung.

### EN

The app requires login. Please provide a reviewer account with owner or manager access.

Some features require device permissions:
- Camera: capture device photos and scan barcode/IMEI
- Photo Library: select and save proof images
- Bluetooth: connect to thermal printers
- Location When In Use: attendance and workplace location verification
- Notifications: receive order, staff, and system event alerts

If the reviewer does not have a Bluetooth thermal printer available, the rest of the application can still be tested normally.
